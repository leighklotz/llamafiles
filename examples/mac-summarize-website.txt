The web page "Curta.Org: Your Complete Curta Calculator Site" is a comprehensive site dedicated to the Curta calculator, a high precision calculating machine. The site offers various resources such as articles, models, manuals, algorithms, programming model, emulators, bulletin board, pictures, news, links, history, commerce, and collaboration.
